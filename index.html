In a move to strengthen the resilience of the nation’s educational infrastructure, the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) today announced that it will provide fire insurance for more than 132,000 public school buildings under the Department of Education (DepEd) nationwide. The total fire insurance for these public school buildings is worth P843.11 billion, and will cover fire, lightning, and natural catastrophes for a one-year period effective from January 1, 2024.
